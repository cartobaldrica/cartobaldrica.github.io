About

The Year of Water is a university-led, statewide initiative to convey the connectedness of water – where it comes from, where it goes, and why we should care. The Year of Water not only highlights the role Oregon’s research universities play as community and industry leaders and partners seeking to understand and address water-related challenges, but also leverages the work of many of Oregon’s public higher education institutions and showcases events of organizations working on behalf of water.

Through this initiative, we aspire to leverage and foster water collaborations within and outside of the universities; increase awareness and coordination to discover synergies across the universities; promote water literacy; and broaden the collective impact and benefits of our work.
