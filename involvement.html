How to be Involved

Criteria for Participating

Broadly interested in the conversation of water in Oregon or around the world in the context of community, culture & the arts, economy, environment, health, safety, and technology & innovation.

● Forward-Looking
● Positive and aimed at mutual understanding
● Focused on innovations

How to Participate

● Follow our Year of Water Facebook site
● Register an event to be posted on the Year of Water Facebook site
● Participate in an event
● Sign up to receive the H2Oregon water newsletter
● Put your research on the Oregon water research tool
● Read a water book or watch a water movie
